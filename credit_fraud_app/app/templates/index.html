Field Explanations

Personal Information

  • Gender: Applicant's gender (M/F)
  • Education: Highest level of education achieved
  • Number of Children: Number of dependent children
  • Age: Applicant's age in years (must be 18 or older)

Assets & Employment

  • Own Car/Property: Whether the applicant owns a car or property
  • Income Type: Source of regular income
  • Years Employed: Duration of current employment

External Source Scores

These are normalized scores from external data sources (range 0-1):

  • Source 1: Credit bureau score
  • Source 2: Alternative credit scoring
  • Source 3: Behavioral scoring

Sample Profiles

Low Risk Profile:

  • Age: 35
  • Income: 75000
  • Years Employed: 8
  • Education: Higher education
  • External Scores: > 0.6

Medium Risk Profile:

  • Age: 25
  • Income: 45000
  • Years Employed: 2
  • Education: Secondary
  • External Scores: 0.4-0.6

How It Works

The application follows these steps:

  1. Data is collected through the form
  2. Features are preprocessed using the same pipeline as training
  3. Model makes a prediction using XGBoost
  4. Results are displayed with risk assessment

Important Notes

  • All fields are required for accurate prediction
  • External source scores significantly impact the prediction
  • The model is regularly retrained with new data
  • Predictions are for informational purposes only
